## Overview

A **Promise** is an object representing the eventual completion or failure of an asynchronous operation. It allows you to attach callbacks to handle the success or failure of the operation, rather than passing callbacks into a function.

<Info>
Promises have three states: **pending** (initial state), **fulfilled** (operation completed successfully), and **rejected** (operation failed).
</Info>
